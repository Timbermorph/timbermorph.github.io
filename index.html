I am a PhD student at the Computer Vision Chair, Technical University of Munich, in the MuMoL group advised by Dr. A. Sophia Koepke. My research centers on multimodal learning, with broad interests spanning text, video, and audio — in particular, how models can perceive, reason about, and learn from context across these modalities.

Research interests: Multimodal Learning (Text, Video, Audio), Vision-Language Models, Large Language Models, In-Context Learning.
