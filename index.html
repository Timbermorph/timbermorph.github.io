I am a PhD student in the MuMoL research group, advised by Prof. Dr. A. Sophia Koepke, jointly affiliated with the Technical University of Munich (Munich Center for Machine Learning) and the Tübingen AI Center. My research centers on multimodal machine learning — in particular, how multimodal large language models perceive, reason about, and learn from visual and textual context.

Research interests: Multimodal Learning, Vision-Language Models, Large Language Models, In-Context Learning.
